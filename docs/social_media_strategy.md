# Social Media Strategy

**Instagram-first approach with Facebook support; influencer partnerships and PR integrated**

---

## Platform Strategy

### Primary: Instagram
- **Audience:** Food lovers, wine enthusiasts, travelers, luxury experience seekers
- **Content pillars:** Food + wine, vineyard, family story, Spanish culture, seasonal highlights
- **Posting frequency:** 3-4x/week (consistent, quality over quantity)
- **Engagement:** Respond to comments within 24 hours; DM engagement for inquiries

**Why Instagram?** Visual platform perfectly suited for food, wine, vineyard, and aesthetics. High intent from travel/dining audiences. Reels reach for discovery.

### Secondary: Facebook
- **Audience:** Slightly older demographic; local Waiheke/Auckland residents; couples and groups
- **Content pillars:** Events, bookings, seasonal announcements, community-focused posts
- **Posting frequency:** 2-3x/week (event-focused)
- **Engagement:** Group discussions, local recommendations, event RSVPs

**Why Facebook?** Booking conversion still strong on Facebook. Local audience engagement. Event promotion.

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Food & Wine (40%)
- Plated dishes (vibrant photography, Spanish techniques visible)
- Wine pours and varietals (sommelier notes)
- Food + wine pairings (storytelling)
- Chef's specials and seasonal menus
- Close-ups of ingredients (garden-to-plate)

**Cadence:** 3-4 posts/week on Instagram; 1-2 on Facebook

### 2. Vineyard & Garden (25%)
- Vineyard views (landscape beauty)
- Seasonal vineyard work (pruning, harvest, flowering)
- Organic garden harvest (fresh produce)
- Winemaking process (behind-the-scenes)
- Terroir storytelling (why Waiheke is special)

**Cadence:** 2-3 posts/week on Instagram; focus on Stories/Reels

### 3. Family Story & Heritage (20%)
- Barnett & Cat (personal moments, philosophy)
- 25+ year journey (milestones, memories)
- Spanish roots and sourcing (travel photos, bodega relationships)
- Team moments (staff, hospitality, community)
- Awards and recognition (celebrate milestones)

**Cadence:** 1-2 posts/week on Instagram; community-focused on Facebook

### 4. Events & Experiences (15%)
- Wine releases and tastings
- Seasonal celebrations (Navidad, Valentine's, etc.)
- Chef's dinners and private events
- Corporate team-building experiences
- Wedding/group packages (testimonials, venue showcase)

**Cadence:** Event-driven; 1-2 announcements/week

## Instagram Content Strategy

### Feed Content
- **Grid aesthetic:** Consistent color palette (Spanish red #D32F2F, wine tones, earthy greens, whites)
- **Photo style:** High-quality, professional food/wine photography; authentic behind-the-scenes moments
- **Captions:** Storytelling (2-3 sentences max); ask questions to drive engagement
- **Hashtags:** 10-15 strategic hashtags per post (local + tourism + food/wine + Spanish)

### Stories (Daily)
- Behind-the-scenes during service
- Ingredient snapshots (garden, Spanish imports)
- Team moments
- Guest experiences
- Polls and questions (engagement)

### Reels (2x/week)
- Quick recipe or technique videos (Spanish cuisine)
- Vineyard timelapse or seasonal work
- Wine pairing tips
- Customer testimonials
- Trending audio with Casita Miro twist

### Carousel Posts (1x/week)
- Wine education (regions, varietals, food pairings)
- Ingredient storytelling (where it comes from, how it's used)
- Event highlights (wedding, corporate, celebration)
- Top 3 wines or dishes (seasonal)

## Influencer & PR Strategy

### Food & Wine Influencers (NZ/Australia)
- Identify 10-15 mid-tier food/wine/travel influencers with 10K-100K followers
- Reach out with personalized press trip offer (vineyard tour + meal + wine tasting)
- Provide talking points (award streak, family story, Spanish authenticity, farm-to-table)
- Monitor mentions and re-share content

**Goals:** 3-5 influencer partnerships/year; aim for 500K+ combined impressions

### Travel & Lifestyle Media
- Target: NZ travel blogs, luxury travel sites, lifestyle magazines (Cuisine, Canvas, NZ Geographic)
- Angle: "Spanish wine escape on Waiheke" or "Hidden gem destination"
- Press release template: 13-year award streak + family story + unique Spanish positioning
- Monthly outreach to 2-3 media contacts

**Goals:** 2-4 media features/year in tier-1 publications

### PR Strategy
- **Award submissions:** Submit to food/wine awards annually (Iconic Eats, Cuisine Magazine, TripAdvisor renews)
- **Press releases:** Wine club launch, seasonal events, Madame Rouge expansion, partnerships
- **Guest content:** Pitch guest articles on Spanish cuisine, wine pairing, farm-to-table approach to food/wine publications
- **Podcast appearances:** Wine/hospitality/entrepreneurship podcasts; tell the family story

**Goals:** 1-2 award nominations + 4-6 PR mentions/year
