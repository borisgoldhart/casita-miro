# Casita Miro - Marketing Plan
**Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Strategy:** Spanish-first positioning, farm-to-table premium, winter revenue growth, product/wine retail expansion

---

## MARKETING OVERVIEW

**Goal:** Position Casita Miro as the definitive Spanish destination in Australasia; drive three revenue streams (restaurant, wine, products) with focus on winter seasonality and e-commerce growth.

**Target Audiences:**
1. **Premium diners** (special occasions, wine enthusiasts) — Waiheke visitors, Auckland CBD professionals
2. **Wine collectors & enthusiasts** — Direct-to-consumer wine club and retail
3. **Food lovers & product buyers** — E-commerce (gift-givers, home cooks, collectors)
4. **Corporate entertainment** — Auckland companies (team-building, client entertainment)
5. **International travelers** — Australasian wine tourism (Australia, NZ)
6. **Spanish cultural community** — Expats, heritage enthusiasts

---

## MARKETING STRATEGY

### 1. BRAND NARRATIVE & STORYTELLING

**Core Story:** Spanish Excellence Meets Waiheke Terroir

**Key Messaging:**
- **"Authentic Spanish on Waiheke soil"** — Own vineyard + organic gardens + direct Spanish sourcing

- **"Farm-to-table with Spanish DNA"** — Local ingredients elevated by Spanish tradition

- Sophisticated old-world (heritage, craftsmanship, authenticity)
- Confident and bold (not fussy or pretentious)
- Warm and welcoming (approachable, community-focused)
