# 🍷 Casita Miro Marketing POC

**Spanish Restaurant + Vineyard | Waiheke Island, New Zealand**

---

## Welcome

This wiki documents the **Marketing Proof of Concept (POC)** for Casita Miro — a premium Spanish restaurant, vineyard, and artisan food producer on Waiheke Island.

### What is Casita Miro?

- **Only Spanish restaurant + own vineyard** on Waiheke Island
- **25+ years** of Mediterranean mastery (Barnett & Cat Bond)
- **Award-winning:** 4.7/5 TripAdvisor, #1 in Onetangi, 13-year award streak
- **Farm-to-table premium:** Organic kitchen gardens + Spanish sourcing + Waiheke terroir
- **Multiple revenue streams:** Restaurant, wine retail, artisan food products, events

---

## POC Objectives

Address Cat's three core business challenges:

1. **Winter slow season revenue** — Convert seasonal dips into year-round revenue through events, e-commerce, and international markets
2. **Sell more kitchen products** — Scale artisan foods (olive oil, olives, honey, walnuts, pesto) to national retail and e-commerce
3. **Sell more wine** — Develop wine club, retail expansion, and direct-to-consumer channels

**Strategic Focus:** Spanish-first positioning (Movida-inspired) with premium farm-to-table differentiation
