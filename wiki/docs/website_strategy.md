# Website Strategy

**Current state review, opportunities, and strategic direction for Casita Miro's digital presence**

---

## CURRENT STATE

### Website Architecture
- **Total pages:** ~25-30 (shop pages, product pages, blog sections)
- **Main sections:** Restaurant (dining, bookings, groups), Vineyard, Events (weddings, groups, Christmas), Shop, Products (Madame Rouge)
- **Functionality:** Online booking, e-commerce shop, email signup, contact forms
- **CMS/Platform:** Custom or modular (Willing & Able credit in footer)

### Current Strengths
✅ Domain owned (good SEO foundation)  
✅ Multiple revenue streams represented (dining, wine, events, weddings, groups)  
✅ Online booking system functional  
✅ E-commerce shop with product filtering  
✅ Separate pages for key experiences  
✅ Email signup (Casita Amigo membership)  
✅ TripAdvisor link (leveraging #1 ranking)  
✅ Social media links present (Facebook, Instagram)  
✅ Founder/family story on Vineyard page  
✅ Clear contact information  

### Current Gaps

| Aspect | Gap | Impact |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Content depth** | No storytelling, no menu detail, no wine list | Low conversion to bookings |
| **Visual content** | No lifestyle imagery, vineyard photos, food content | Misses emotional connection |
| **SEO** | No keyword-targeted content, no blog | Poor search visibility for "Spanish restaurant Waiheke" |
| **UX/Navigation** | Unclear CTA hierarchy, hidden menus | Friction in booking journey |
| **Trust signals** | No customer testimonials, no about/team bios | Relies entirely on off-site reviews |
| **Wine program** | Not detailed, no list or tasting notes | Misses wine-focused visitors |
| **Social integration** | Email signup only | Missing engagement channels |

### Missing Content

**Pages/Sections NOT on website:**
- ❌ Full wine list with tasting notes
- ❌ Complete menus with descriptions and pricing
- ❌ Food photography or lifestyle imagery
- ❌ Pricing for events, groups, dining
- ❌ Wedding or corporate event packages
- ❌ Chef or team bios
- ❌ Restaurant history and origin story
- ❌ Awards details and links
- ❌ Blog/news/content marketing
- ❌ FAQ or customer guides
- ❌ Video tours or virtual experiences
- ❌ Parking, directions, accessibility details

### Current Traffic Sources

**Where visitors find them:**
1. TripAdvisor (likely primary — 1,758 reviews, #1 ranking)
2. Waiheke tourism sites
3. Google search ("restaurants Waiheke")
4. Word of mouth

**Missed opportunities:**
- Not visible in wine tourism searches
- Not leveraging food blogger/influencer channels
- Not capturing email outside "Casita Amigo" program
- No content marketing for organic traffic

## STRATEGIC PRIORITIES

### Priority 1: Content & Storytelling
- Tell the Casita Miro story (Barnett & Cat's journey, 25+ years)
- Showcase food, wine, vineyard through quality imagery
- Create pages that communicate value clearly
- Build trust through customer testimonials and awards recognition

### Priority 2: Menus & Pricing Transparency
- Display full Fiesta menu with descriptions
- Show pricing for dining, events, cellar door
- Create wedding/group packages with clear pricing
- Build wine list with tasting notes and sommelier insights

### Priority 3: SEO & Organic Discovery
- Target keywords: "Spanish restaurant Waiheke," "wine experience Waiheke," "farm-to-table," etc.
- Create blog content on Spanish cuisine, wine pairings, Waiheke travel
- Build internal linking structure
- Optimize meta tags and page titles

### Priority 4: User Experience
- Clear path to bookings from homepage
- Simplified navigation with intuitive CTAs
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast load times and clean aesthetics

### Priority 5: Email & Community
- Expand Casita Amigo newsletter (regular content, wine releases, events)
- Add blog to support newsletter growth
- Integrate social media presence
- Build feedback loop for customer insights
