## Conclusion

Casita Miro has a **reasonably comprehensive website** with multiple revenue streams represented (dining, wine sales, events, groups). However, the **content is thin, scattered, and not optimized** for discovery or conversion. Key pages lack detail, pricing, imagery descriptions, and persuasive copy. The shop is sophisticated but isolated from the storytelling on other pages.

**Content richness ratio:** Content exists across ~25-30 pages, but most pages are skeletal compared to competing luxury experiences.
