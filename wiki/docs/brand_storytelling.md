# Brand & Storytelling

**Spanish-First Positioning with Farm-to-Table Premium**

---

## Core Positioning

**"Authentic Spanish on Waiheke Soil"**

- Spanish-first (not Mediterranean fusion)
- Farm-to-table premium (organic gardens + Spanish sourcing + Waiheke terroir)
- Award-winning heritage (13-year TripAdvisor streak, family-run)
- Movida-inspired aesthetic (bold, sophisticated, welcoming)

---

## Key Messages

- **25+ years of Mediterranean mastery** — Barnett & Cat's credibility
- **Own vineyard + organic gardens + direct Spanish sourcing** — Integrated supply chain
- **Award-winning since 2011** — 13-year TripAdvisor award streak validates quality
- **Spanish-first, not fusion** — Authentic cuisine and ingredients

---

## Brand Personality

- Sophisticated old-world (heritage, craftsmanship, authenticity)
- Confident and bold (not fussy or pretentious)
- Warm and welcoming (approachable, community-focused)
- Curated expertise (deep knowledge of Spanish food and wine)
