# Marketing Plan Overview

**Spanish-First Positioning, Farm-to-Table Premium, Winter Revenue Growth**

---

## Marketing Goals

**Primary Goal:** Position Casita Miro as the definitive Spanish destination in Australasia; drive three revenue streams with focus on winter seasonality and e-commerce growth.

**Success Definition (12 Months):**
- Winter revenue = 60% of summer (vs. current 40-50%) → **+20-25% annual revenue**
- Wine revenue = 25-30% of total → **+10-15% growth**
- Product revenue = 15-20% of total → **+10-20% growth**
- **Total potential uplift: +40-60%**

---

## Target Audiences

1. **Premium Diners** — Special occasions, wine enthusiasts, Waiheke visitors, Auckland CBD professionals
2. **Wine Collectors** — Direct-to-consumer wine club, retail, international customers
3. **Food/Gift Buyers** — E-commerce, home cooks, collectors, gift-givers (especially winter holidays)
4. **Corporate Entertainment** — Auckland companies, team-building, client entertainment
5. **International Travelers** — Wine tourism, Australasian visitors, vacation planners
6. **Spanish Community** — Expats, heritage enthusiasts, cultural community

---

## Marketing Channels & Strategies

### 1. **Brand Narrative & Storytelling**

**Core Positioning:** "Authentic Spanish on Waiheke soil"

**Key Messages:**
- 25+ years of Mediterranean mastery (Barnett & Cat's credibility)
- Own vineyard + organic gardens + direct Spanish sourcing
- Award-winning since 2011 (13-year streak validates quality)
- Spanish-first, not fusion (authentic cuisine and ingredients)
- Farm-to-table with Spanish DNA (local + global authenticity)

**Brand Personality:** Sophisticated old-world, confident & bold, warm & welcoming, curated expertise

---

### 2. **Website & Digital Presence** (Critical Priority)

**Current State:** Skeletal pages; no menus, pricing, imagery, storytelling

**Redesign Goals:**
- Showcase Spanish identity (visual + copywriting)
- Drive bookings, e-commerce, email signups
- SEO optimization for key terms
- Clear revenue stream discovery

**Key Pages to Build:**
- Homepage: Value proposition, awards, CTAs (book, shop, wine club, learn)
- Restaurant/Dining: Full Fiesta menu, prices, cellar door, reservations
- **Wine:** Club details, shop catalog, sommelier notes, Miro Vineyard story
- **Products:** Gallery, sourcing stories, e-commerce, gift sets, subscriptions
- Farm-to-Table: Garden tours, seasonal storytelling, Spanish sourcing
- About/Story: Barnett & Cat's 25-year journey, awards, team
- Events/Corporate: Wedding packages, team-building, private dining
- Blog: Wine guides, cooking traditions, seasonal content, pairing guides

---

### 3. **Social Media Strategy**

**Platforms:** Instagram (primary), Facebook (community), LinkedIn (corporate)

**Content Breakdown:**
- Food (40%) — Fiesta plating, tapas, ingredient prep, seasonal highlights
- Wine (25%) — Wine pours, vineyard, winemaking, tasting notes, sommelier tips
- Products (15%) — Olive oil, olives, honey, Madame Rouge, gift sets, unboxing
- Behind-the-Scenes (10%) — Kitchen, garden, team, events, Barnett's mosaic
- Stories (daily) — Day-in-the-life, customer content, events, releases

**Frequency:** 3-4 posts/week Instagram, 2-3 Stories/day, 2-3 Facebook posts/week

**Visual Aesthetic:** Movida-inspired (bold, sophisticated, Spanish color palette, high-quality food photography)

---

### 4. **Email Marketing**

**Email Programs:**
- **Casita Amigo Newsletter** (2x/month) — Seasonal updates, wine releases, products, events, recipes
- **Wine Club Emails** (Monthly + quarterly) — Release previews, tasting notes, pairings, exclusive offers
- **Product/Shop Emails** (2x/month) — New products, gift guides, bundles, stock updates
- **Corporate/Events** (As needed) — Event highlights, testimonials, seasonal packages

**List Building:** Segment existing Casita Amigo list, grow through website signups, product purchases, wine club

---

### 5. **Content Marketing (Blog + Educational)**

**Blog Topics (1-2 posts/week):**
- Wine Education — Spanish wine regions (Rioja, Andalusia, Basque), pairing guides
- Food Culture — Spanish culinary traditions, tapas history, seasonal cooking
- Farm Stories — Garden seasonality, organic practices, ingredient sourcing
- Recipes — Traditional Spanish dishes, Casita Miro signatures, product use guides
- Events — Seasonal celebrations, festival traditions, chef collaborations
- Behind-the-Scenes — Vineyard operations, kitchen, team stories, Barnett's mosaic

**Goals:** SEO optimization, educational authority, email list growth, social content repurposing

---

### 6. **Influencer & Partnership Strategy**

**Influencer Tiers:**
- Food/Wine creators (50K-500K followers) — Farm tours, exclusive tastings, chef's experiences
- Travel bloggers — Multi-hour experiences (dinner + vineyard tour + tasting)
- Wine educators — Wine club partnerships, educational content collaboration
- Spanish/Cultural influencers — Authentic storytelling, cultural celebration events

**PR Partnerships:**
- Food/Wine Publications (Cuisine, Vogue Dining, Good Food)
- Travel Media (Lonely Planet, Condé Nast Traveller, NZ travel guides)
- Wine Industry (publications, sommelier networks, education platforms)

**Ideas:** Press trips for journalists (farm + dinner + wine), co-created content, festival partnerships

---

### 7. **Advertising & Paid Media**

**Paid Search (Google Ads):**
- Keywords: "Spanish restaurant Waiheke," "wine experience," "fine dining," "farm-to-table," "wine club"
- Landing pages: Restaurant booking, wine club, product shop
- Budget: Seasonal boost (summer, winter events, holiday shopping)

**Social Media Ads (Facebook/Instagram):**
- Restaurant: Target Waiheke day-trippers, special occasions, wine interest
- Wine Club: Wine enthusiasts, gift-givers (winter), premium audiences
- Products: Food/gift interest, premium buyers, Waiheke tourism
- Events: Corporate entertainment, Auckland CBD professionals

**Budget Allocation:** 40% Search, 40% Social, 15% Influencer, 5% Other

---

### 8. **Events & Experiential Marketing**

**Year-Round Programming:**
- Spring: Harvest celebrations, vintage tastings, spring menu launches
- Summer: Peak season, weekly events, guest dinners, special experiences
- Autumn: Wine release parties, seasonal ingredient celebrations
- Winter: Chef's dinners, corporate events, holiday parties, wine education

**Monthly/Quarterly Events:** Wine club tastings, sommelier education, chef's table, seasonal collaborations, cultural celebrations

---

### 9. **Partnerships & Distribution**

**Waiheke Tourism:**
- Partner with Waiheke Tourism Bureau (co-marketing)
- Get listed in visitor guides and tourism websites
- Ferry operator packages (ferry + lunch + tasting)
- Cross-promote with galleries, wineries, accommodation

**National Retail:**
- Specialty food stores (Moore Wilson, Nadene's)
- Online food platforms (local retailers, subscription boxes)
- Wine retailers and wine clubs
- Department stores (merchandise collaborations)

**International:**
- Wine export partnerships (Australia, UK, USA)
- International wine club promotion
- Luxury travel operators and tourism sites

---

### 10. **Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**Q1 (Jan-Mar): Summer Peak**
- Heavy restaurant promotions
- Summer events and celebrations
- Product gift bundles (Australia holiday season)
- Wine club Q1 releases

**Q2 (Apr-Jun): Autumn Transition**
- Wine release parties
- Harvest celebrations
- Seasonal menu content
- Corporate team-building ramp-up

**Q3 (Jul-Sept): Winter Focus** ⭐ **CRITICAL**
- Winter event programming emphasis
- Wine club expansion (Northern Hemisphere holiday gift prep)
- Holiday season product campaigns
- Corporate entertainment packages
- E-commerce promotions

**Q4 (Oct-Dec): Holiday Peak**
- Holiday gift guides and gift sets
- Gift card promotions
- Bundle and subscription promotions
- Year-end corporate events
