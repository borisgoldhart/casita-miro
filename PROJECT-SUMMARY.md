## The Challenge (Cat's Three Problems)

### 1. Winter Slow Season Revenue
**Problem:** Restaurant/wine/products generate 40-50% of annual revenue in peak summer months (Nov-March are slow), despite being peak tourism season globally.

**Solution:** Year-round event programming (wine releases, chef's dinners, corporate team-building), covered cellar door operation, and international e-commerce targeting Northern Hemisphere holiday season.

**Target:** 60% of summer revenue in winter (vs. current 40-50%) → **+20-25% annual uplift**

### 2. Untapped Kitchen Product Sales
**Problem:** Artisan products (olive oil, olives, honey, walnuts, pesto) sold only to local vendors; no retail or e-commerce strategy; <5% of revenue.

**Solution:** Redesign on-site shop as destination, launch e-commerce, develop national retail partnerships (specialty food shops, gourmet retailers), create gift sets and subscriptions.

**Target:** 15-20% of revenue from products (vs. current <5%) → **+10-20% growth**

### 3. Underdeveloped Wine Business
**Problem:** Wine retail ~15% of revenue; no wine club, limited online presence, minimal e-commerce; missing direct-to-consumer and international opportunities.

**Solution:** Launch 3-tier wine club ($75-250/quarter; recurring revenue), curated wine shop with sommelier notes, e-commerce with international shipping, sommelier-led content marketing.

**Target:** 25-30% of revenue from wine (vs. current ~15%) → **+10-15% growth**

## Strategic Direction

### Brand Positioning
**"Authentic Spanish on Waiheke Soil"**

- **Spanish-first** (not Mediterranean fusion)
- **Farm-to-table premium** (organic gardens + Spanish sourcing + Waiheke terroir)
- **Award-winning heritage** (13-year TripAdvisor award streak, family-run)
- **Movida-inspired aesthetic** (bold, sophisticated, welcoming)

### Three Interlocking Revenue Streams

**1. Restaurant (Year-Round with Events)**
- Premium Fiesta menu: $85-110/person
- Casual cellar door: $15-40/person
- Events: weddings, corporate, private dining
- Winter strategy: Event programming, covered operation, corporate team-building

**2. Wine (Club + Retail + E-Commerce)**
- Wine club (3 tiers): $75-250/quarter
- Curated wine shop + e-commerce
- Sommelier-led education and content
- International shipping and marketing
- Target: 25-30% of total revenue

**3. Kitchen/Food Products (Retail + Online)**
- Scale artisan foods: oils, olives, honey, walnuts, pesto
- National specialty food retail partnerships
- E-commerce direct-to-consumer
- Gift sets and subscription boxes
- Branded merchandise (ceramics, glassware, cookbooks)
- Target: 15-20% of total revenue

## Marketing Strategy Summary

### Website Redesign Blueprint
Complete redesign detailed including all key pages:
- Homepage (value proposition, awards, clear CTAs)
- Restaurant/Dining (full Fiesta menu, prices, cellar door)
- Wine (club details, shop, sommelier notes, vineyard story)
- Products (gallery, sourcing, e-commerce, gift sets)
- Farm-to-Table (garden tours, seasonal storytelling)
- About/Story (Barnett & Cat's 25-year journey)
- Events/Corporate (weddings, team-building, private dining)
- Blog (wine guides, cooking traditions, pairing)

### Social Media Strategy
- **Platform:** Instagram primary (3-4 posts/week + daily Stories)
- **Content Mix:** 40% food, 25% wine, 15% products, 10% behind-scenes
- **Visual Aesthetic:** Movida-inspired (bold, sophisticated, Spanish palette)

### Email Marketing
- **Casita Amigo Newsletter** (2x/month) — seasonal updates, wine releases, products, events
- **Wine Club Emails** (monthly + quarterly) — releases, tastings, exclusive offers
- **Product/Shop Emails** (2x/month) — new products, gift guides, bundles
- **Corporate/Events** (as needed) — highlights, testimonials, seasonal packages

### Content Marketing
- Blog strategy (1-2 posts/week) targeting SEO keywords
- Wine education (regions, pairing guides, production)
- Food culture (Spanish traditions, seasonal cooking, recipes)
- Farm stories (garden seasonality, organic practices)
- 50+ blog topic ideas included

### Paid Advertising Budget
- Annual: $47K-81K (~$4K-6.7K/month)
- Google Ads (40%): Search marketing
- Facebook/Instagram (40%): Social advertising
- Influencer partnerships (15%): Reach and credibility
- Photography/Video (5%): Content creation
