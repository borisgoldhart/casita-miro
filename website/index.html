25+ Years of Spanish Mastery

Barnett and Cat Bond created Casita Miro from a passion for authentic Spanish food, exceptional wine, and creating unforgettable moments. What began as a small restaurant has evolved into one of New Zealand's most celebrated culinary destinations.

Every detail—from the vineyard's terroir to the sourcing of Spanish delicacies—reflects their commitment to authenticity, quality, and the art of hospitality.
