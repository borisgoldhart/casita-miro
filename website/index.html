A Spanish Dream on Waiheke

Three things make Casita Miro extraordinary. First, we make our own award-winning wines from our 4.2-hectare vineyard—a complete control over our terroir and craft. Second, we grow our own fresh produce in organic gardens, harvesting daily for our menus. Third, we source the best-of-best Spanish ingredients directly from Spain's renowned bodegas and artisanal producers.

It's not just a restaurant. It's a complete world of Spanish culture, food, wine, and hospitality on one stunning Waiheke property.
