Sourcing & Sustainability

Spanish Sourcing

Our partnership with bodega families in Andalusia spans 15+ years. We work directly with producers, ensuring fair prices, sustainable practices, and products we genuinely love. Every olive oil bottle, every jar of olives, every batch of jamón comes with personal relationships and deep respect for tradition.

Local Organic Gardens

Our Waiheke producers grow chemical-free, biodynamically managed gardens. We support three local farms and apiaries that share our commitment to the land and ethical food production.

Farm-to-Table Philosophy

The kitchen doesn't just cook—it transforms. We create signature products only when we have ingredients at their peak. Madame Rouge's recipe was perfected over five years. Nothing is rushed or compromised.

Our Commitments

🌱 Sustainability
No artificial chemicals, minimal packaging, compostable where possible.

✋ Fair Trade
Direct relationships with producers. Fair prices that respect their expertise and labor.

🏡 Local First
Waiheke Island producers get preference. Supporting community comes first.

🍇 Quality Always
We refuse to compromise. If it's not exceptional, we don't sell it.
