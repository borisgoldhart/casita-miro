Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a dining reservation?

You can book online through our Dining page, call us at +64 9 372 5025, or email hello@casitamiro.co.nz. We confirm all reservations within 2 hours. Walk-ins welcome at Cellar Door (no booking required).

What are your opening hours?

Summer (Nov-Mar): Thursday-Sunday, 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Autumn-Spring (Apr-Oct): Friday-Sunday, 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Closed Mondays. Seasonal closures apply in winter.

Do you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes! We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-free requests. Please notify us when booking so our kitchen can prepare accordingly.

What's your cancellation policy?

Reservations can be cancelled up to 48 hours before your booking with no penalty. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a cancellation fee equal to 50% of the total booking value.

Can we bring children?

Absolutely! Children are welcome at Casita Miro. We recommend the Cellar Door for families (more casual) but Fiesta menu reservations work too. Let us know when booking and we'll ensure a great experience for everyone.

Do you offer group discounts?

For groups of 6+, contact us directly to discuss options. We can often arrange special group pricing and customized experiences.

Can I pre-order wine for my visit?

Yes! Contact us before your visit and we can set aside bottles or prepare specific wine pairings. Just mention this in your reservation notes or call us directly.

Is there a dress code?

Smart casual is recommended. No beachwear or athletic wear. Come as you are—we focus on making you feel comfortable and welcomed.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, we have wheelchair accessible parking and entrance. Please contact us if you have specific accessibility requirements and we'll ensure everything is perfect for your visit.

More questions? Contact us directly at +64 9 372 5025 or hello@casitamiro.co.nz
