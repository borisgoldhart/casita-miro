Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for dinner service and weekends. Walk-ins welcome, but availability may be limited.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer vegetarian tasting menus and can accommodate most dietary requirements. Please let us know in advance.

Can I purchase your wines online?

Contact us directly for wine orders and shipping. Wine Club members receive quarterly selections and discounts.

What's the dress code?

Smart casual. We're relaxed but refined. Beachwear and thongs are not appropriate for evening service.
