The Art of Spanish Dining

Farm-to-table excellence where tradition meets island terroir

Our Philosophy

At Casita Miro, dining is not simply about eating—it is about experiencing the soul of Spain through the lens of Waiheke Island. Every plate tells a story of place, craft, and passion.

We believe that the best food begins in the garden. Our organic kitchen gardens produce fresh herbs, vegetables, leafy greens, citrus, and seasonal produce harvested daily. This isn't just farm-to-table rhetoric; it's our commitment to absolute freshness and flavor.

We source Spanish ingredients directly from bodegas and artisanal producers we've cultivated relationships with over years. Jamón, aged cheeses, conservas, premium oils, and wines arrive regularly from our partners in Spain, maintaining the authenticity that defines Spanish cooking.

The result is Spanish cuisine that is genuine, contemporary, and rooted in the Waiheke terroir that has become home to this dream.

The Menu

Our menu changes seasonally, reflecting what grows in our gardens and what arrives from our Spanish suppliers. Each season brings new discoveries and renewed traditions.

Lunch Service

Our lunch menu features lighter dishes perfect for the island's warm days. Fresh salads, grilled fish, jamón and cheese boards, and Spanish wines by the glass.

Dinner Service

The Gaudí Bar

A stunning focal point in the restaurant, our Gaudí-inspired mosaic bar is where the magic of Spanish culture comes alive. Adorned with intricate tilework and bold colors, it's not just a bar—it's a celebration of the artistry and passion that defines Spanish hospitality.

Here, you'll discover our curated selection of Spanish wines, spirits, and our exclusive Madame Rouge aperitif. Each drink is crafted with intention, each conversation is intimate, and each moment is unforgettable.

The bar is open during all dining hours and welcomes drop-in guests for wine, aperitifs, and conversation.
