Our Dining Philosophy

Farm-to-table Spanish cuisine, executed with precision and passion

Seasonal, Fresh, Authentic

Every dish at Casita Miro begins in our organic gardens, where we cultivate fresh herbs, vegetables, and citrus year-round. Each morning, our kitchen team harvests the day's ingredients — guaranteeing maximum freshness and flavor.

We combine New Zealand's bounty with direct imports from Spain's finest bodegas and artisanal producers. The result is a menu that honors Spanish tradition while celebrating local terroir.

Our Gaudí-inspired mosaic bar — a stunning focal point in the restaurant — sets the stage for intimate conversations and unforgettable moments.
