Dining at Casita Miro

Award-winning Spanish cuisine. Farm-to-table philosophy. Exceptional service with ocean views.

Book a Table View Menu

Our Dining Philosophy

At Casita Miro, we believe exceptional dining comes from authenticity, quality, and care. Every dish celebrates Spanish tradition while showcasing the best of Waiheke Island's produce.

Farm-to-table approach: We work with local organic growers to source seasonal ingredients at their peak.

Spanish sourcing: Our pantry is filled with carefully curated Spanish imports—olive oils, cured meats, cheeses, and wines.

Service philosophy: We treat every guest like family. Hospitality is at the heart of everything we do.

Fiesta Menu

Our signature 3-course tasting menu, featuring seasonal Spanish cuisine and exceptional ingredient sourcing.

First Course

Jamon Ibérico & Burrata

Spanish cured ham, creamy burrata, heirloom tomatoes, aged balsamic

Sourced from a family bodega in Andalusia, our Jamon represents 36 months of tradition and expertise.

Espuma of Local Mushroom

Wild mushrooms from Waiheke, white truffle oil, crispy bread

A celebration of autumn produce paired with Spanish olive oil from our estate vineyard region.

Main Course

Pan-Seared Snapper with Romesco

Fresh local snapper, red pepper & almond sauce, seasonal vegetables

Romesco—a Spanish classic from Catalonia. Our version uses red peppers from Waiheke organic farms.

Slow-Roasted Lamb Shoulder

Free-range lamb, saffron rice, piquant olive tapenade

Cooked low-and-slow for 8 hours. Served with our house-made Spanish spices and finishing salt.

Dessert

Torrijas (Spanish Cinnamon French Toast)

Brioche, cinnamon custard, dulce de leche, toasted almonds

A Spanish Easter tradition served year-round. Pan-fried and served warm with a dusting of cinnamon.

Chocolate & Churro

Dark Spanish chocolate, fried dough, sea salt

Our take on Madrid's beloved churrerías. Rich chocolate and crispy pastry—simple and perfect.

Price: NZD $185 per person | Wine pairing: NZD $85 (5 wines selected by Barnett)

Dietary requirements: We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and allergy-free menus. Please notify when booking.
