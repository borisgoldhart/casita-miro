Aperitivo Hour

An intimate wine tasting experience with seasonal Spanish pintxos

A Tasting Like No Other

Aperitivo Hour is a cosy, playful tasting designed for wine lovers, curious travelers, and anyone seeking a memorable night on Waiheke. Join us for an intimate pre-dinner experience in our Glass House overlooking the vineyard, where rare wines meet seasonal Spanish pintxos.

The Experience

Your host guides you through three exclusive wines from our vineyard, each paired with a seasonal pintxo crafted fresh in our kitchen. This isn't just a tasting—it's a journey through flavors, stories, and the passion that defines Casita Miro.

We close the evening with a wander to the Mosaic Bar for a final toast and stunning sunset views before you continue to dinner.

What's Included

  • Three exclusive Miro wines
  • Three seasonal Spanish pintxos
  • Host guidance and storytelling
  • Access to Mosaic Bar
  • Final toast with views
