Our Story

A Spanish dream realized on Waiheke Island

Casita Miro: A Vision Realized

Casita Miro began as a dream—a desire to create a destination that captures the passion, culture, and authenticity of Spain while celebrating the unique terroir of Waiheke Island. Today, it stands as one of New Zealand's most celebrated restaurant and wine experiences.

Barnett Bond

Winemaker & Vineyard Manager

Barnett brings decades of winemaking expertise and a deep passion for viticulture. His commitment to quality over quantity, and his understanding of Waiheke's terroir, has made our vineyard one of the island's most respected producers. Under his stewardship, we produce wines that are authentic, distinctive, and built to last.

Cat Bond

Restaurant Manager & Chef Leadership

Cat's vision for the restaurant is rooted in authenticity, precision, and care. She has cultivated a kitchen team dedicated to Spanish traditions while embracing Waiheke's fresh, local ingredients. Her leadership ensures that every meal at Casita Miro is an unforgettable experience—not just about food, but about hospitality and connection.

Awards & Recognition

For 13 consecutive years, we've earned TripAdvisor's Traveller's Choice award. We're ranked #1 in Onetangi and recognized by critics and food writers as one of New Zealand's most distinctive dining destinations.

Our Values

Authenticity

We don't imitate Spanish culture—we honor it. Our commitment to genuine Spanish ingredients, techniques, and hospitality is unwavering. Every dish, every wine, every experience reflects our respect for Spanish tradition.

Quality

We believe in doing fewer things better. Whether it's vegetables from our gardens, wines from our vineyard, or service at our tables, excellence is non-negotiable.

Place

Waiheke Island is not just where we are—it's who we are. Our food, wines, and ethos are rooted in this place. We celebrate local terroir while honoring Spanish heritage.

Connection

Food and wine are about bringing people together. Whether it's a intimate dinner or a celebration with friends, we create moments that matter.
