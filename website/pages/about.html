Our Story

25+ years of passion, excellence, and authentic Spanish hospitality. The Casita Miro journey.

A 25+ Year Journey

Barnett and Cat Bond created Casita Miro from a shared passion: authentic Spanish food, exceptional wine, and the art of hospitality. What began as a small restaurant on Waiheke Island has evolved into one of New Zealand's most celebrated culinary destinations.

Their commitment to quality has never wavered. Every dish celebrates Spanish tradition. Every wine is tasted and approved by hand. Every guest leaves feeling like family.

This isn't a business for them—it's a way of life.

The Owners

Barnett Bond

Winemaker & Vineyard Manager

Barnett's journey into winemaking began during family travels through Spain in his 20s. He studied viticulture and winemaking, learning from tradition-bound bodegas in Rioja and Catalonia. In 2001, he brought that expertise to Waiheke Island, planting his first vineyard and committing to the craft.

His winemaking philosophy is simple: minimal intervention, natural fermentation, quality over quantity. He tastes every vintage personally before release. His wines have earned international recognition and formed the foundation of Casita Miro's reputation.

Passion: Winemaking, Spanish viticulture, storytelling

Cat Bond

Restaurant Operations & Chef Leadership

Cat's culinary journey started in her grandmother's kitchen and led her across Spain, working in family-run restaurants and learning the secrets of authentic Spanish cooking. She returned to New Zealand with a mission: bring the warmth, flavors, and spirit of Spanish hospitality to Waiheke Island.

She's built a team of talented chefs and created a hospitality experience where every guest feels welcomed and cared for. Her Madame Rouge aperitif—a secret blend inspired by Andalusia—has become an iconic product. She believes great food and wine bring people together.

Passion: Spanish cuisine, hospitality, creating connections

Our Values

Authenticity

Spanish heritage is at our core. We don't chase trends. We honor traditions, respect our suppliers, and stay true to what made us fall in love with this culture in the first place. Every decision filters through the lens of authenticity.

Community

Hospitality means treating people like family. We build relationships with our suppliers, our staff, our guests. Casita Miro isn't a transaction—it's a gathering place. Every person who walks through our doors matters.

Quality

Excellence in every detail. The ingredients we source. The wines we age. The service we provide. We refuse to compromise. If something isn't exceptional, we don't offer it. Awards follow naturally when you commit to quality.

Sustainability

We care about the land and the people who work it. Organic farming practices. Fair prices for suppliers. Minimal packaging. Local-first sourcing. We're custodians of Waiheke Island's beauty and its future.
