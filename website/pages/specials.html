Meet the Makers

A seasonal experience exclusive to winter months. Join Casita Miro's winemakers for an intimate, behind-the-scenes exploration of our craft, rare vintages, and the stories that shape our vineyard.

The Experience

Go beyond tasting. Sit down with the people who make Casita Miro wines. This intimate gathering brings winemakers and passionate wine lovers together for honest conversation, rare wine selections, and stories from the vineyard.

What's Included

  • Direct maker engagement — Meet the winemakers who crafted your wines
  • Rare vintages — Taste limited-edition and single-barrel selections not available elsewhere
  • Behind-the-scenes stories — Learn the decisions, challenges, and triumphs of each vintage
  • Vineyard insights — Understand Waiheke's terroir and how it shapes our wines
  • Gourmet pairing — Spanish-inspired small bites paired with the wines
  • Intimate setting — Limited to 12 guests for genuine connection

$195 per person
