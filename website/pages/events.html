Types of Events

Wine Release Events

Quarterly celebrations of new vintages. Taste the latest releases, meet Barnett, enjoy canapés prepared by our chef, and take home bottles at release pricing.

  • ✓ Frequency: Quarterly (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter)
  • ✓ Duration: 4 hours
  • ✓ Price: Free entry | Wine by the glass
  • ✓ What's Included: Tasting, canapés, live music, 10% discount on bottle purchases
  • ✓ Best For: Wine club members, local wine enthusiasts, tourists seeking authentic experiences
Book Wine Release

Cultural Celebrations

Seasonal Spanish celebrations: Navidad (Christmas), Three Kings Day, Valentine's Day Spanish-style, Fiesta de la Tomatina (summer). Authentic food, wine, music, and storytelling.

  • ✓ Events: Navidad, Three Kings, Valentine's, Tomatina Fiesta
  • ✓ Duration: 4-5 hours
  • ✓ Price: NZD $45-95 per person
  • ✓ What's Included: Multi-course meal, wines, entertainment, cultural storytelling
  • ✓ Best For: Families, friend groups, anyone seeking authentic Spanish culture
Reserve Celebration

Chef's Dinners

Intimate dinners for 8-12 people. Custom menu designed by our chef, wine pairings selected by Barnett, and an evening of exceptional food and conversation.

  • ✓ Group Size: 8-12 people
  • ✓ Duration: 3.5 hours
  • ✓ Price: NZD $280-350 per person
  • ✓ What's Included: 4-course menu, wine pairings, chef's introduction
  • ✓ Best For: Special occasions, milestone celebrations, culinary enthusiasts

Corporate Team-Building

Custom experiences for your team. Vineyard tours, cooking classes, wine tastings, and team lunches. Build connection while enjoying world-class hospitality.

  • ✓ Group Size: Up to 40 people (multiple seatings)
  • ✓ Options: Vineyard tour, cooking class, wine tasting, team lunch
  • ✓ Price: Custom quotes (typically NZD $85-180/person)
  • ✓ Duration: 3-5 hours depending on activity
  • ✓ Best For: Company outings, off-sites, team bonding

Weddings & Private Functions

Your wedding deserves a beautiful setting and exceptional hospitality. We accommodate intimate ceremonies (30 people) to larger celebrations (60 people) with customized menus and wine selections.

  • ✓ Capacity: 30-60 guests (tiered options)
  • ✓ Options: Ceremony + reception, reception only
  • ✓ Price: NZD $155-220 per person (all-inclusive)
  • ✓ Includes: Venue, catering, wine, service, coordination
  • ✓ Best For: Intimate weddings, civil ceremonies, receptions, renewal celebrations

Let's Plan Your Event

Our Process

  1. Inquiry: Tell us your vision—date, size, budget, preferences
  2. Consultation: We discuss options, menu ideas, wine pairings, pricing
  3. Proposal: Detailed proposal with timeline and next steps
  4. Confirmation: Deposit secures your date
  5. Coordination: Regular check-ins leading up to your event
  6. Execution: Your perfect event comes to life
  7. Follow-up: We'd love to hear from you—feedback helps us improve
