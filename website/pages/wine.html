Our Vineyard

4.2 hectares. Award-winning wines. 13 years of excellence.

Waiheke Terroir

Our estate vineyard occupies 4.2 hectares of prime Waiheke Island land, where warm, sun-blessed days are tempered by cooling coastal winds. This unique microclimate produces grapes of genuine distinction.

From the first vine planted, we committed to producing wines of exceptional quality—not volume. Every vintage reflects the character of the land and the vintage year. We work closely with our grapes, understanding their potential, coaxing out their best.

The wines produced here have earned consistent recognition: 13 years of TripAdvisor Traveller's Choice awards reflect not just our restaurant's reputation, but our vineyard's commitment to excellence. Wine critics and collectors recognize our Syrah as one of the finest from Waiheke Island.
