Our Vineyard

4.2 hectares of Waiheke terroir. Award-winning wines. 13 years of excellence.

From Vine to Glass

Our estate vineyard sits on 4.2 hectares of prime Waiheke Island land, where we cultivate exceptional grapes that capture the island's unique terroir — warm, sun-blessed days tempered by cooling coastal winds.

We produce a curated selection of wines, including our celebrated Syrah, elegant Rosé, and other varietals that consistently earn recognition from critics and collectors worldwide.

Each bottle tells the story of our land, our passion, and our commitment to producing wines of genuine distinction.
