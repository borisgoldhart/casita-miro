Miro Vineyard Story

Our 4.2-hectare vineyard sits on Waiheke Island's distinctive volcanic soils, where Pacific breezes and long sunshine hours create optimal growing conditions.

Terroir

Waiheke Island's unique terroir—volcanic soils, maritime climate, and 2,000+ hours of annual sunshine—produces wines of remarkable character and depth. Our location mirrors some of the world's greatest wine regions, allowing us to craft bottles that rival international standards.

Winemaking Process

Barnett Bond's winemaking philosophy emphasizes minimal intervention and natural fermentation. We harvest by hand, ferment in temperature-controlled vessels, and age in French oak. Each vintage is tasted and approved by Barnett before release.

Varietals

  • Syrah – Full-bodied, spiced, berry-forward
  • Cabernet Sauvignon – Bold, structured, age-worthy
  • Merlot – Smooth, elegant, food-friendly
  • Sauvignon Blanc – Crisp, herbaceous, refreshing
  • Chardonnay – Buttery, complex, mineral
