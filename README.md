## 📍 About Casita Miro

An award-winning Spanish restaurant and vineyard on Waiheke Island, New Zealand.

- **Speciality:** Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine, fine wine, shared dining experiences

## 🎯 Workstreams

Following the **House of Eros POC model**, we're executing four key phases:

1. **As-Is Analysis** - Current digital presence, customer journey, competitive landscape
2. **Business Strategy Update** - Market opportunity, positioning, revenue streams
3. **Marketing Plan** - Campaign themes, channels, seasonal calendar
4. **Website Concept** - Redesign recommendations, UX/UI, content strategy

**Next Steps:** As-Is Analysis + Business Strategy Update
