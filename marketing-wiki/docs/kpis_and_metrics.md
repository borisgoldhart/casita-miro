# KPIs & Metrics

**Success indicators and performance targets for Casita Miro marketing and business strategy (12-month horizon)**

---

## Overview

Success is measured across **four dimensions:**
1. **Revenue & Profitability** — Financial performance of each stream
2. **Customer Acquisition & Retention** — Growth and loyalty metrics
3. **Brand & Awareness** — Market position and reputation
4. **Operational Excellence** — Execution and customer satisfaction

## Monthly Review Cadence

### Week 1: Collect Data
- Revenue (restaurant, wine, products, events)
- Website analytics (traffic, conversion, referral sources)
- Email metrics (opens, clicks, conversions, unsubscribes)
- Social media analytics (followers, engagement, reach)
- Customer feedback (reviews, NPS, survey responses)

### Week 2: Analyze & Interpret
- Compare to targets and previous month
- Identify trends (up, down, flat)
- Diagnose what's driving changes
- Flag anomalies or concerns

### Week 3: Strategic Discussion
- Review findings with leadership (Barnett, Cat, marketing lead)
- Discuss what's working, what needs adjustment
- Plan next month's tactical adjustments
- Prioritize growth areas

### Week 4: Implement & Report
- Adjust marketing strategy or tactics based on findings
- Share results with team
- Celebrate wins; document lessons learned
- Set focus for upcoming month

---

## Quarterly Business Review

**Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 Review (End of Quarter):**

1. **Overall performance** — Did we hit targets? By how much?
2. **Revenue analysis** — Which streams outperformed? Any red flags?
3. **Acquisition cost** — CAC by channel (website, email, social, PR, partnerships)
4. **Retention** — Repeat rates, churn, lifetime value improvements
5. **Brand health** — Awareness metrics, PR, reputation trends
6. **Operational excellence** — Quality, customer satisfaction, team performance
7. **Strategic adjustments** — What to double down on, what to sunset, what's new

---

## Annual Strategic Review

**Year-End Review (December):**

1. **Annual revenue** — Actual vs. target across all streams
2. **YoY growth** — Compared to prior year, industry benchmarks
3. **Customer metrics** — Total customers, repeat rate, lifetime value, NPS
4. **Marketing ROI** — Investment per channel vs. revenue generated
5. **Brand progress** — Awards, PR, reputation, awareness growth
6. **Operational learnings** — What worked, what didn't, key lessons
7. **Next year strategy** — Updated targets, new initiatives, resource allocation

## Key Success Indicators (KSIs) to Watch Continuously

**Top 5 metrics that indicate overall health:**

1. **Total revenue** — All streams combined; primary profitability indicator
2. **Winter revenue %** — Measures seasonal challenge being solved
3. **Customer repeat rate** — Indicates loyalty and product-market fit
4. **Email subscriber growth** — Signals marketing effectiveness and list quality
5. **Wine club members** — Recurring revenue model health; high-margin business

**If any of these decline, activate escalation process:**
- Diagnose root cause within 5 days
- Propose solution within 10 days
- Implement within 30 days
- Monitor weekly until recovery

## Dashboard & Reporting Tools

### Recommended Tools
- **Google Analytics** (website traffic, conversion, referral sources) — free
- **Mailchimp Analytics** (email performance) — free tier
- **Instagram/Facebook Insights** (social engagement, reach) — free
- **Google Data Studio** (unified dashboard) — free; visualize all data
- **Spreadsheet tracker** (manual revenue entry, monthly review) — Google Sheets

### Dashboard at a Glance
Should show:
- Revenue (monthly, YTD, YoY comparison)
- Website traffic (sessions, conversion, top referral sources)
- Email (subscribers, open rate, click rate)
- Social (followers, engagement rate, reach)
- Customer metrics (repeat rate, average order value, NPS)
- Key strategic indicators (all green, yellow, or red)
