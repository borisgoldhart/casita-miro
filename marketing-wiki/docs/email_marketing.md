# Email Marketing

**Building Casita Amigo into a powerful subscriber-driven loyalty and sales channel**

---

## Email Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Transform Casita Amigo email list from sign-up only into a structured, valuable communication channel that drives bookings, wine sales, events, and community.

**Approach:** Segmented email flows based on customer behavior (diners, wine club, product buyers, event attendees) with consistent storytelling, offers, and education.

---

## Email List Building

### Current Baseline
- Casita Amigo sign-up on website
- Event and booking inquiry capture
- Estimated current subscribers: 300-500

### Growth Targets (12 months)
- 1,000+ email subscribers (2-3x growth)

### Acquisition Channels
1. **Website signup** (Homepage, footer, sidebar CTAs)
2. **In-restaurant signup** (QR code on tables, menu inserts; incentive: 10% off wine purchase)
3. **Event attendees** (Automatic add after booking confirmation)
4. **Social media** (Instagram bio link to signup form)
5. **Checkout incentive** (Online shop: "10% off next purchase if you subscribe")
6. **Wi-Fi signup** (If installed; email required to connect)

---

## Email Segmentation

### Segment 1: Diners
- **Who:** Recent restaurant visitors (booked Fiesta menu)
- **Frequency:** 2x/month
- **Content:** Menu specials, wine pairings, seasonal celebrations, event invitations
- **Goals:** Repeat bookings, wine retail cross-sell

### Segment 2: Wine Club Members
- **Who:** Paid wine club subscribers
- **Frequency:** Weekly (releases, tasting notes) + monthly (shipping, exclusive offers)
- **Content:** New releases, producer stories, pairing notes, exclusive tastings, member-only events
- **Goals:** Retention, repeat purchases, community building

### Segment 3: Product Buyers
- **Who:** Purchased food products (olive oil, olives, honey, Madame Rouge)
- **Frequency:** 2-3x/month
- **Content:** New product releases, recipes, usage guides, seasonal gift bundles, wholesale partnership updates
- **Goals:** Repeat purchases, gift giving, seasonal revenue spikes

### Segment 4: Event/Wedding Inquiries
- **Who:** Viewed wedding/group pages or made inquiry
- **Frequency:** 1-2x/week (nurture sequence, then monthly)
- **Content:** Package details, testimonials, planning guides, booking incentives
- **Goals:** Conversion to bookings, premium pricing

### Segment 5: Occasional Visitors
- **Who:** Signed up but haven't booked recently
- **Frequency:** 1x/month
- **Content:** "What's new at Casita Miro," seasonal highlights, re-engagement offers
- **Goals:** Reactive activation, reduce churn

## Email Content Types

### 1. Weekly Newsletter (Wine Club + General Subscribers)
**Day:** Tuesday or Wednesday  
**Length:** 300-400 words  
**Subject:** "[New Release] Miro Winter 2026 Pinot Gris — Tasting Notes Inside"

**Structure:**
- Intro (personal from Barnett or Cat; 1-2 sentences)
- Featured content (wine release, seasonal highlight, or recipe)
- Quick tip or story (2-3 paragraphs)
- CTA (book tasting, shop product, or view full recipe)
- Footer (social links, contact info, unsubscribe)

### 2. Monthly Promotions (All Segments)
**Timing:** First or third Tuesday of month  
**Length:** 150-250 words  
**Subject:** "Spring Garden is Here — New Seasonal Menu + 15% Wine Club Exclusive"

**Structure:**
- Hero image (garden, vineyard, seasonal photo)
- Headline (seasonal theme)
- Promotion details (what's new, discount code, expiration)
- CTA (book now / shop / view menu)

### 3. Event Announcements (Targeted)
**Timing:** 4 weeks, 2 weeks, 1 week before event  
**Length:** 200-300 words  
**Subject:** "Wine Release Party — Thursday, June 15 | Miro Vineyard"

**Structure:**
- Event headline and date/time
- Description (what to expect, why it matters)
- Details (location, duration, pricing, what to bring)
- CTA (RSVP / book / register)
- Q&A (common questions answered)

### 4. Educational Content (Wine/Food)
**Timing:** 2x/month (alternating weeks with newsletter)  
**Length:** 400-600 words  
**Subject:** "Spanish Sherries 101 — A Sommelier's Guide to Fino, Amontillado, and Oloroso"

**Structure:**
- Introduction (why this topic matters)
- Main content (detailed guide, history, or techniques)
- Recipe or pairing example
- Related products (link to shop for featured wines/foods)
- CTA (book a tasting / attend an event)

### 5. Customer Testimonials & Stories (Engagement)
**Timing:** 1x/month  
**Length:** 300-400 words  
**Subject:** "From First Visit to Wedding Venue — Emma & James's Casita Miro Story"

**Structure:**
- Headline (guest name, experience type)
- Story (quotes from guest, emotional moment, why Casita Miro mattered)
- Details (what they experienced, menu, wine, special requests honored)
- CTA (book now / view wedding packages / contact for custom event)

### 6. Seasonal Campaigns
**Timing:** Before major seasons (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter)  
**Length:** Variable (can be short promotional or longer storytelling)

**Examples:**
- **Winter (May-Aug):** Covered cellar door + event programming + wine club holiday gift bundles
- **Spring (Sept-Nov):** Harvest celebrations + new releases + garden rotation
- **Summer (Dec-Feb):** Outdoor season + tourism marketing + food product gift guides
- **Fall (Mar-May):** Spring transitions + new menus + festival season

## Email Flows & Automation

### 1. Welcome Series (New Subscriber)
**Trigger:** Email signup confirmation  
**Duration:** 3 emails over 1 week

- **Email 1 (Immediate):** Welcome + confirm subscription + intro to Casita Miro story (1 min read)
- **Email 2 (Day 3):** "Why Spanish?" — Barnett & Cat's philosophy + vineyard story (2 min read)
- **Email 3 (Day 7):** Exclusive offer — "10% off your first booking or wine purchase" (code expires 14 days)

### 2. Post-Booking Confirmation Series
**Trigger:** Restaurant booking confirmed  
**Duration:** 4 emails over 14 days

- **Email 1 (Immediate):** Booking confirmation + details + what to expect + wine pairing preview
- **Email 2 (Day 3):** Menu preview (if Fiesta) + wine options + dietary notes
- **Email 3 (Day 7):** "Excited?" — Testimonial from past guest + directions + parking tips
- **Email 4 (Post-Visit):** Thank you + request for review + exclusive wine discount (7 days expiry)

### 3. Post-Event Engagement (Events/Weddings)
**Trigger:** Event attendance confirmed  
**Duration:** 3 emails over 30 days

- **Email 1 (Event day):** Reminder + itinerary + what to bring
- **Email 2 (Day 2):** Thank you + photos (if available) + event recap
- **Email 3 (Day 14):** Follow-up offer — "Book your next celebration" + 10% discount on future events

### 4. Win-Back Campaign (Inactive Subscribers)
**Trigger:** No opens or clicks for 60 days  
**Duration:** 2 emails over 2 weeks

- **Email 1:** "We miss you!" — What's new at Casita Miro + seasonal highlight + 15% comeback offer
- **Email 2:** Last chance message — Final incentive + link to unsubscribe (optional; respect preference)

## Email Metrics & Goals

### Engagement Targets (12 months)
- **Open rate:** 25%+ (industry average for restaurants is 20-25%)
- **Click-through rate:** 3-5% (links to bookings, shop, events)
- **Unsubscribe rate:** <0.5% (low; indicates valuable content)
- **Conversion rate:** 2-3% of opens result in booking or purchase

### Business Impact Targets
- **Email-driven bookings:** 50+ per month (10-15% of total reservations)
- **Email-driven wine sales:** $1,000+/month (10-15% of wine retail revenue)
- **Event sign-ups:** 20-30% from email campaigns
- **Product purchases:** 100+/month from email prompts

## Design & Brand Consistency

### Email Template Elements
- **Header:** Casita Miro logo + 🍷 emoji + tagline
- **Brand colors:** Spanish red #D32F2F for highlights, white background, dark text
- **Font:** Clear, readable sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica) for body; bold serif for headlines
- **Image placement:** Hero image at top or mid-email (vineyard, food, family photo)
- **Mobile responsive:** Single column layout; thumb-friendly CTAs
- **Footer:** Social icons + contact info + unsubscribe link (required by law)

### CTA Buttons
- Color: #D32F2F (Spanish red)
- Text: Action-oriented ("Book Now," "Shop Wine," "View Recipe," "RSVP")
- Placement: Mid-email and at end (2-3 CTAs max)

## Email Tools & Platforms

### Recommended Platforms
- **Mailchimp** (free up to 500 contacts; adequate for starting)
- **Substack** (simple, newsletter-focused; good for educational content)
- **Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)** (advanced automation, transactional emails, affordable)

### Setup Requirements
- Email list import + segmentation
- Template design (can use provided template or hire designer)
- Automation workflows (welcome series, post-booking, win-back)
- Analytics dashboard (track opens, clicks, conversions)
