# Casita Miro - Marketing POC Requirements

## Overview
Marketing proof of concept for **Casita Miro**, an authentic Spanish restaurant and vineyard located on Waiheke Island, New Zealand.

**Website:** https://www.casitamiro.co.nz/

**Location:** 3 Brown Road, Onetangi, Waiheke Island 1081, NZ

## Business Context

**Casita Miro** specializes in Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine with an award-winning wine program. They operate:
- **Restaurant:** Lunch daily (11am), dinner Saturdays
- **Cellar Door:** À la carte tapas and wine tastings (lunch only, weather permitting)
- **Shared dining model:** Tapas and raciones (large plates) for social experiences

### Recent Accolades
- TripAdvisor Traveller's Choice "Best of the Best" (2011-2024)
- Iconic Eats Auckland Top 100 (2025)
- Lewisham Awards Winner - Outstanding Local Establishment (2025)
- Cuisine Magazine Good Food Awards (2024)

## Project Scope

This POC follows the **House of Eros model** with four key workstreams:

### 1. As-Is Analysis
- Current website evaluation
- Digital presence audit
- Customer journey mapping
- Competitive landscape (Waiheke restaurants/wineries)
- Messaging and positioning analysis

### 2. Business Strategy Update
- Market opportunity assessment
- Brand positioning refinement
- Customer segmentation
- Revenue streams analysis (dine-in, cellar door, events, online wine sales)
- Growth opportunities

### 3. Marketing Plan
- Campaign themes and messaging
- Channel strategy (social, email, partnerships, local marketing)
- Seasonal marketing calendar
- Customer acquisition strategy

### 4. Website Concept
- Redesign recommendations
- UX/UI improvements
- Feature prioritization
- Content strategy
- Technical recommendations

---

## Goals

- Establish a clear marketing strategy and roadmap
- Create a compelling website concept that reflects Casita Miro's premium positioning
- Identify growth opportunities in the hospitality space
